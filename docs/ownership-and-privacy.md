# Ownership, permission, and privacy

Aboubakr Ibrahim completed this work as a paid freelance biomedical research and MATLAB contribution. His contribution includes STFT/ISTFT implementation, CNN experimentation, audio processing, evaluation, visualization, and technical reporting.

The recording category is speech and respiratory sounds. Publication permission has been confirmed by the contributor. Raw recordings are nevertheless excluded from this public repository to reduce privacy and redistribution risk. Client identity and unnecessary personal metadata remain confidential.

Only anonymized examples with documented consent and no identifying speech content should ever be added publicly.
